What are Hyenas?

Hyenas are carnivorous mammals of the family Hyaenidae, closely related to cats but exhibiting dog-like hunting behaviors.

About Hyenas

Hyenas are highly intelligent carnivorous mammals known for their powerful jaws and complex social structures. Despite their reputation as scavengers, many hyena species are skilled hunters that play a critical role in their ecosystems.
